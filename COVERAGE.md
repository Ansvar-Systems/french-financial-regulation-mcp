# Corpus Coverage

This document describes the regulatory corpus indexed by the French Financial Regulation MCP server.

## Sourcebooks

| Sourcebook ID | Authority | Full Name | Coverage |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| `AMF_Reglement_General` | AMF | Règlement Général de l'AMF | Books I–VI: market operations, intermediaries, collective investment, market infrastructure, market abuse, financial information |
| `AMF_Positions` | AMF | Positions-Recommandations AMF | DOC-YYYY-NN series: supervisory positions and recommendations on market conduct, disclosure, and product governance |
| `AMF_Doctrine` | AMF | Doctrine AMF | Interpretive guidance, Q&As, and thematic reviews published by AMF divisions |
| `ACPR_Instructions` | ACPR | Instructions ACPR | Numbered instructions governing prudential reporting, AML/CFT, governance, and operational requirements for credit institutions and insurers |
| `ACPR_Recommandations` | ACPR | Recommandations ACPR | Non-binding best-practice recommendations issued by ACPR on risk management, customer protection, and internal controls |

## Regulatory Bodies

### AMF — Autorité des marchés financiers

- **Role:** Supervision of financial markets, investment services providers, collective investment schemes, and market transparency
- **Official portal:** <https://www.amf-france.org>
- **Règlement Général:** <https://www.amf-france.org/fr/reglementation/reglement-general>
- **Doctrine (positions, recommandations):** <https://www.amf-france.org/fr/reglementation/doctrine>

### ACPR — Autorité de contrôle prudentiel et de résolution

- **Role:** Prudential supervision of banks, insurance companies, and payment institutions; AML/CFT enforcement
- **Official portal:** <https://acpr.banque-france.fr>
- **Instructions:** <https://acpr.banque-france.fr/reglementation/instructions>
- **Recommandations:** <https://acpr.banque-france.fr/reglementation/recommandations>

## Topic Areas Covered

- Market abuse and manipulation (abus de marché, manipulation de cours)
- Investment services and suitability (évaluation de l'adéquation, services d'investissement)
- Collective investment schemes (OPC, OPCVM, FIA)
- Market infrastructure and post-trade (infrastructure de marché, post-marché)
- Financial disclosure and transparency (information financière)
- AML/CFT requirements (LCB-FT)
- Prudential requirements for banks and insurers
- Customer protection and conduct of business
- Corporate governance and internal controls
- Sanctions and enforcement decisions

## Coverage Limitations

- Coverage is **periodic** — data is ingested from official publications and may lag by days to weeks.
- Translations are not provided; provisions are stored in French as published.
- Informal guidance, press releases, and consultation papers are not included unless formally adopted as doctrine.
- Historical versions of amended provisions may not be fully indexed.

Verify critical references against primary sources at <https://www.amf-france.org> and <https://acpr.banque-france.fr>.
